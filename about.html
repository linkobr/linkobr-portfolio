About Me:

Hi there! My name is Link O'Brennan and my pronouns are he/they. I am a research assistant and an undergraduate student at the University of Colorado at Boulder. I am currently studying Women and Gender Studies and Anthropology with a minor in Japanese and a certificate in LGBTQ studies. I am a queer and trans feminist scholar, passionate about archiving, teaching, and writing our histories; a cultural anthropologist interested in studying how the development of culture and society inform our behavior and our futures, and a social justice advocate who seeks to challenge systems and hierarchies of oppression and exploitation and work to secure a just future for all.
